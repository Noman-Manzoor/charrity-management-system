    Our Vision

    This Donation management system enables every person to donate easily. In this modern era, it is quite difficult and inefficient way for charity organizations and NGO’s to do on-door collection. Today, time is passing like waves while many people can’t donate due to time limitations. This platform helps people to select preferred charity organizations and send the donation to them easily by sitting at their homes. It also help NGO’s to collect donations online instead of door to door collections. In other words, the overall aim of this web system is to increase online donations and minimize the wastage of time.
